Have you found an orphaned or injured wild animal that needs help?

In this situation, you need to locate the closest state-licensed wildlife rehabilitator near you who works with the species.

Personally, I rehabilitate our New York-native predator-type mammals (foxes, minks, weasels). Please note that I CANNOT always intake an animal, as I am a one-man show with wildlife rehab, and I have capacity limitations.

The Animal Help Now website has been a GREAT resource for connecting the public and rehabbers. Type in your address, and it will populate a list of rehabbers based on your location. Remember, not all rehabbers are licensed for all species.

Or, do you need help with a captive-bred exotic?

Need placement for a captive-born fox, wolfdog, or other exotic pet or rescue? Include details about the species, age, vet history, temperament, and photos of the animal in your email. Please note I may not always have capacity to intake your animal.

Note for Wolfdog/Wolf Hybrid Placement:

Many Wolfdog sanctuaries are often at full capacity. Consider posting on Wolfdog rehoming groups on Facebook, where qualified homes actively monitor posts. Use your discretion to vet potential homes.
